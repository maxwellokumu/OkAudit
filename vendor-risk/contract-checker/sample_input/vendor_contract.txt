MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Acme Corp ("Customer")
and CloudVendor Ltd ("Vendor") as of January 1, 2025.

1. SERVICES
Vendor agrees to provide cloud-based data processing and analytics services as described
in the applicable Statement of Work. Services shall be delivered in accordance with
industry standards and best practices.

2. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
Vendor guarantees a system availability of 99.5% uptime measured on a monthly basis,
excluding scheduled maintenance windows. In the event of downtime exceeding the SLA,
Customer shall be entitled to service credits equal to 10% of monthly fees for each
hour of excess downtime, up to a maximum of 30% of the monthly fee.

3. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's proprietary
information and trade secrets. Confidential information shall not be disclosed to
third parties without prior written consent. This obligation survives termination
of the Agreement for a period of five (5) years.

4. DATA SECURITY AND BREACH NOTIFICATION
Vendor shall implement and maintain appropriate technical and organisational measures
to protect Customer data against unauthorised access, loss, or destruction. In the
event of a data breach or security incident, Vendor shall notify Customer within
forty-eight (48) hours of discovery. Vendor holds SOC 2 Type II certification,
which is reviewed annually by an independent auditor.

5. DATA PROCESSING
Vendor shall process Customer data solely in accordance with Customer's documented
instructions and for no other purpose. Vendor agrees to delete or return all personal
data upon termination of this Agreement. Customer retains all ownership of its data
throughout the term of this Agreement.

6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement for cause upon thirty (30) days written
notice if the other party materially breaches the Agreement and fails to cure such
breach within the notice period. Upon termination, Customer may export its data
in standard formats for a period of sixty (60) days.

7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
In no event shall either party's total liability exceed the fees paid by Customer
in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. This limitation of liability applies
to all claims, including negligence, breach of contract, and breach of warranty.
Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of
England and Wales. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction
of the courts of England and Wales.

9. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All intellectual property developed by Vendor in connection with the services shall
remain the property of Vendor. Customer retains all rights to its data and any
pre-existing intellectual property provided to Vendor.

10. SUBCONTRACTORS
Vendor may engage subcontractors to perform certain services under this Agreement,
provided that Vendor remains responsible for the performance of such subcontractors.
Customer consent is required before engaging any new subcontractor with access to
Customer data.

11. GENERAL
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Any amendments
must be in writing and signed by both parties. If any provision is found unenforceable,
the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
